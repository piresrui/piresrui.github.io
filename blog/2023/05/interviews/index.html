Interviews


After a round of layoffs at Anchorage, inevitably I had to get back to interviewing.

Just the subtle reminder at how broken interview processes are. From companies asking for 5-10 interview rounds, to ghosting, to no feedback, to delays in offers, to leetcode puzzles, to bad interviewers.

Nerve wracking. Thankfully I've been able to find some companies that understand how broken the system is and try to improve within it's constraints.
